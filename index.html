latest

The group announces in 2022 that they will be focusing on individual activites for the time being. Media outlets blew the news out of proportion stating that the group was taking a hiatus. The reality is the older group members needed to have time to serve the military forces in their home country. They determined the best course of action was to continue solo activities and pause on group activities while the members serve their national duty. Currently, the oldest member, Jin has enlisted in the military and is serving his mandatory 2 years. Other members have been busy with their individual activities.

RM: Released his solo album 'Record of RM: Indigo'

Jin: Prior to his military service he released a single called ' The Astronaut' produced by Cold Play

Suga: Released a collab with Singer Psy called 'That, that.' He is also preparing to go on a solo tour.

J-Hope: Released solo album 'Jack in the Box.' He performed at the New Year's Rockin' Eve in Times Square as well as at Lollapalooza.

Jimin: Released a collab with Singer Taeyang called 'VIBE'

V: Is starring in a new variety series called 'Jinny's Kitchen'

Jung Kook: Featured in Charlie Puth's 'Left and Right.' Opened for the Fifa World Cup 2022 with the new single 'Dreamers.'
